Decoding My Playlist

Have you ever wondered what your playlist reveals about you? Let me take you on a journey through the meticulous curation process of my playlist, a collection that reflects not just my taste but the stories and emotions intertwined with each melody.

How do I curate my playlist?

My meticulously curated playlist is a reflection of my musical identity. Each addition undergoes a rigorous 4-listen evaluation before earning "liked" status and potential playlist inclusion. I also keep a journal logging all song recommendations from others—treating each suggestion as invaluable inspiration for self-discovery.
