How do I curate my playlist?

My meticulously curated playlist is a reflection of my musical identity. Each addition undergoes a rigorous 4-listen evaluation before earning "liked" status and potential playlist inclusion. I also keep a journal logging all song recommendations from others—treating each suggestion as invaluable inspiration for self-discovery. Exploring the Features of my playlist

Spotify has this cool API from which you can get the audio features of your Music. here are some features I gathered:

  • Acousticness: Measures acoustic elements' presence.
  • Danceability: Rates the track's dance suitability.
  • Energy: Gauges a track's intensity and activity.
  • Speechiness: Detects spoken words presence.
  • Valence: Rates the positivity of the music.
  • Liveness: Indicates live performance probability.
  • Instrumentalness: Predicts vocal absence.
